Your Journey to Responsible Driving Starts Here.

Turbo Course is a **professional driving education institution** committed to providing structured, safe, and comprehensive training programs for aspiring drivers.

With an emphasis on **road safety**, confidence building, and modern instructional techniques, Turbo Course ensures that every participant develops the required competence to drive responsibly. We prioritize high-quality instruction delivered by certified trainers equipped with industry experience.

Our Background & History

**Established in 2020**, Turbo Course was founded by automotive and road-safety practitioners who recognized the growing need for standardized driving instruction in Indonesia.

Beginning with a single training unit, Turbo Course expanded through a **reputation for excellence**, structured curricula, and strong commitment to safety. Today, it is recognized as a **trusted training center** supporting both private individuals and corporate clients.

Vision, Mission, and Core Values

Vision

To become Indonesia’s leading driving training institution that produces **safe, skilled, and responsible drivers**.

Mission

  • To deliver high-quality and accessible training programs.
  • To promote road safety as the foundation of every training activity.
  • To maintain professionalism through certified instructors and well-maintained vehicles.
  • To integrate technology into the learning experience, including digital evaluation tools.
  • To foster a culture of discipline and respect for traffic rules.

Core Values

Safety First Professionalism Trust Integrity Continuous Learning

Products & Services

Turbo Course offers comprehensive training programs designed to meet diverse needs:

Regular Driving Course (Beginner Program)

Vehicle control, basic maneuvering, parking, and on-road practice.

Intensive / Fast-Track Course

Short-duration program for learners with limited time availability.

Driving Test Preparation (SIM A)

Theory lessons and practical simulations of official driving tests.

Defensive Driving Program

Advanced techniques for hazard anticipation and safety awareness.

Pick-up and Drop-off Service

Convenient transportation service for all participants.

SWOT Analysis

Strengths

  • Certified instructors with extensive industry experience.
  • Well-maintained training vehicles and modern teaching methods.
  • Flexible scheduling and professional customer service.

Weaknesses

  • Premium pricing compared to informal driving schools.
  • Service coverage limited to major urban areas.

Opportunities

  • Growing public demand for professional driving education.
  • Potential partnerships with automotive companies and corporate fleets.
  • Development of digital learning platforms and simulators.

Threats

  • Increased competition from low-cost driving schools.
  • Rising fuel and maintenance costs.
  • Potential regulatory changes affecting training operations.
